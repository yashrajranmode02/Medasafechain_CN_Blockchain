Help & FAQs

How do I upload OMR sheets?+

Go to Dashboard → Upload, select image or PDF of OMR sheets, and click Upload. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, PDF.

How are scores and percentages calculated?+

Each OMR sheet is evaluated against the answer key. Correct answers increase the score. Percentage = (Score ÷ Total Questions) × 100.

What does Pass/Fail mean?+

By default, a score of 40% or above is marked as Pass. Below 40% is Fail. The threshold can be customized by the institution.

OMR not recognized properly?+

Ensure the sheet is fully visible and scanned clearly. Avoid blurry photos or shadows. For handwriting, ask students to write roll numbers inside boxes clearly.
